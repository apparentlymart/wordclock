- fünf vor halb drei -- five until half to three (twenty-five past two)
- halb drei -- half to three (half past two)
- fünf nach halb drei -- five after half to three (twenty five to three)
...so in German the "halb" must go after the "vor" and "nach",
   and luckily we don't need to fit "fünfundzwanzig", which is too long to fit
